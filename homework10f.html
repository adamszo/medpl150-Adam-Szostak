Homework 10: Interactive Story Assignment


You call out to the eye “Who are you?”It looks at you intrigued. You don’t hear an answer but what can you expect it is but a lone eye. Until you start to feel the hairs on your neck raise. You hear an ethereal voice but you don’t know if it’s in your head or in the void. It says “Who are you?”. You wait there confused. Should you answer first? You asked the question first after all. But as you start to think you can’t seem to come to an answer. Who are you? As you start thinking about the question deeper you realize, you don’t know where you are. The void looks different now. The eye is gone. But in this new place filled with people. You are stuck with the question. “Who are you?”.
